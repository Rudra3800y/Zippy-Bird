Welcome to Zippy Bird – A Fun Flappy Bird-Inspired Game

Zippy Bird is a fun, fast-paced browser game inspired by the classic Flappy Bird. Your goal is simple: tap or click to make the bird fly and dodge the pipes. Sounds easy? Think again! Zippy Bird tests your reflexes and focus in a challenging way that's both fun and addictive.

Whether you're a casual gamer or a high-score chaser, Zippy Bird is perfect for a quick break or a serious challenge. The more you play, the better your score! Compete with your friends and show off your skills.

No downloads, no ads between every tap — just pure gameplay. Try it now and see how far you can go!
