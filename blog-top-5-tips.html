1. Find Your Rhythm

Every player develops a unique rhythm. Start slow, find your natural tapping speed, and stick with it as the game speeds up.

Keeping Zippy in the middle of the screen gives you more time to react to upcoming pipes and adjust your position smoothly.

3. Don't Tap Too Much

Over-tapping will make your bird fly too high and crash into the top pipe. Tap just enough to stay afloat.
